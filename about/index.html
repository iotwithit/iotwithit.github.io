Why am I doing this?

Because I like digital computing systems, getting to understand their founding principles.

And I love to learn about the ways we use these artificial elements to rationalize the physical world.
To derive data to control it, closing the loop.

And I am intrigued by the Internet of Things as the attempt to merge the physical and digital realities: the biggest digital network connecting sensible objects, resulting in a whole new Cyber-Physical system.

Forgive me for the buzzwords, but I think they are the right ones to use in a proper context.

What this?

The this I am referring to in this page is the IoT withit project.

I am an Embedded Software Engineer with a Robotics and Automation Master’s Degree.
Feel free to go to my LinkedIn page (and ask for a connection) to see what I work with, and what my experiences are.

IoT withit is the attempt to share the knowledge I gained, to create a network of people sharing my Whys and purpose. A network of people looking for advice on a project/business they are developing or a career they are building.

Follow me, as we say these days, but, more importantly, interact, suggest, ask.
